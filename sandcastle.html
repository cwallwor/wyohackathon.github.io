SANDCASTLE INVITATIONAL CHALLENGE

September 22, 2019 12pm - 5pm

Some winners of WyoHackathon 2019 will join other vetted blockchain startups in the Sandcastle Invitational Challenge.

Sandcastle is seeking the best and most promising blockchain startups the world over to compete during Dubai2020 in the finale Sandcastle Challenge.  Two winners from the Wyoming Blockchain Stampede will earn two of the 16 slots to compete in EXPO2020 Dubai.
